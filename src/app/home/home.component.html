Welcome to Faith Baptist Church!

We encourage, teach, and strengthen fellow Christians in a balanced, biblical approach to ministry telling the good news of Christ's salvation to those who do not know.

Our Foundation

 We hold to the fundamentals of the faith based upon the always relevant, unchanging foundation of Biblical truth. God's Word holds a prominent place in our activities and services.

Our Motto

 Faith Baptist Church is best summed up by our motto:

  • To Know Christ and Make Him Known

Our Desire

 We want to be seen as a group of people who are continually striving to be like Jesus Christ in our personal lives, in our love for others, and in our church.

Our Invitation

 Whether you are looking for a church home, searching for answers, or just curious, know that you are welcome at our church where we will point you to Jesus Christ.

What Makes Us Different?
  • We are conservative and traditional, yet fresh and alive.
  • We are burdened about having a genuine, thriving walk with God.
  • We are trying to educate and equip, not entertain in our services.
  • We are willing to take a stand against sin.
  • We desire to help build godly lives, families, and friendships.
  • We preach and apply the Bible in an accurate, practical way.

Where will you spend eternity?
  • The Bible provides us with the assurance that we can be right with God and be with Him forever. Consider Jesus' words to a religious man: "Except a man be born again, he cannot see the kingdom of God" (John 3:3)
  • The Damage Done by Sin
    We are all sinners because we have broken God's Law (James 2:10, 1 John 3:4). Our sins have separated us from God, placing us under His righteous wrath and the penalty of sin, which is eternal death and damnation.
  • The Helplessness of Man
    No amount of good works (baptism, communion, etc.) could ever atone for our sin. We are saved only by God's grace, through faith in His Son, Jesus Christ, who alone is "the Way, the Truth, and the Life" (John 14:6).
  • The Good News from God
    Christ died for our sins, was buried, and rose again! It is faith in the finished work of Jesus that is the only basis for soul's salvation (John 1:12).
    Upon what are you depending on this very moment to secure your eternity with God? The Scripture says, "now is the day of salvation" (2 Corinthians 6:2). We urge you to repent of your sin and false trust, turn to Jesus Christ and place your full trust in Him today.
