Welcome to Faith Baptist Church!

We encourage, teach, and strengthen fellow Christians in a balanced, biblical approach to ministry telling the good news of Christ's salvation to those who do not know.

Our Foundation

 We hold to the fundamentals of the faith based upon the always relevant, unchanging foundation of Biblical truth. God's Word holds a prominent place in our activities and services.

Our Motto

 Faith Baptist Church is best summed up by our motto:

  • To Know Christ and Make Him Known

Our Desire

 We want to be seen as a group of people who are continually striving to be like Jesus Christ in our personal lives, in our love for others, and in our church.

Our Invitation

 Whether you are looking for a church home, searching for answers, or just curious, know that you are welcome at our church where we will point you to Jesus Christ.
